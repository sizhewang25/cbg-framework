\subsection{Constraint Preprocessing}
\label{sec:framework-preprocessing}
% TODO
\textit{[Phase 2 subroutines that filter/weight before intersection:
(1) Redundant-disk filter — drops VPs whose disk is contained by another (Octant);
(2) Inside-all filter — retains only VPs whose band contains the joint interior point;
(3) Constraint weighting — scales each annulus by its VP's LTD fit quality (Octant/Spotter);
(4) Octant hull adjustment — on empty intersection, relax the inner annulus bound.
Key failure mode: one tight band remaining after filtering nulls the entire intersection
(documented in~\cite{finding_spherical_circle_brittle}).]}

\subsection{Fallback Paths}
\label{sec:framework-fallback}
% TODO
\textit{[When the joint intersection is empty (EMPTY\_REGION), variants fall back in one of two ways:
(a)~loosen the tightest constraint and re-intersect (Octant hull adjustment);
(b)~return the shortest-ping VP location directly (Vanilla/Million-scale FALLBACK).
The fallback decision is variant-specific and is the primary driver of the EMPTY\_REGION
class in the §\ref{sec:results-failure} taxonomy.
Speed-of-Internet is the only variant that cannot produce EMPTY\_REGION by construction
($r_v \geq 0$ always), because its bands are upper-bound disks with no inner annulus.]}
