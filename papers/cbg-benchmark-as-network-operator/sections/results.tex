\subsection{Success and Failure Analysis}
\label{sec:results-failure}
\textit{[Two-layer failure taxonomy: (A) geometric partition of MTL region vs. truth disk:
EMPTY\_REGION, EXCLUSIVE\_REGION, INCLUSIVE (success or misclassification);
(B) phase attribution (P1 band residuals, P2 participant geometry, P3 centroid rule).
Key finding: EXCLUSIVE is large for annulus variants --- Octant $\approx$50\%, Spotter $\approx$89--99\%
(Shapely polygon-disk intersection, verified on official outputs/).
Per-VP band check was insufficient: each VP's annulus may reach D\_truth, but the joint
intersection can drift entirely beyond D\_truth. Classification accuracy survives via
\emph{EXCLUSIVE-but-correct} centroid snapping: Octant's entire 27\,pp tolerance dividend
comes from $\approx$27/48 EXCLUSIVE predictions that still snap to the right answer cell;
Spotter's 6.3\% accuracy is entirely EXCLUSIVE-but-correct.
Disk variants (Vanilla, Million-scale): EXCLUSIVE $\approx$0--14\%; failure is INCLUSIVE misclassification.
Feature attribution: (1)~VP proximity primary; (2)~answer-space isolation (\texttt{nearest\_other\_centroid\_km}
AUC 0.64--0.68); (3)~angular surround (\texttt{part\_circ\_var} AUC up to 0.82 in DE, regime-gated).
Confidence flag: L1 (region in exactly one cell) precision 0.66--0.91, covers 30--56\% correct answers.
Figure: failure attribution stacked bars (both regimes); per-feature AUC boxplot.]}

\subsection{Production Cost}
\label{sec:results-cost}
\textit{[Cost is entirely in the centroid phase. Monte Carlo medoid (Octant/Spotter): 190--390\,ms/target,
3.2\,targets/s. Geometric centroid: $\approx$0.25\,ms/target, 65\,targets/s.
$\approx$20$\times$ throughput gain; equal or better accuracy (\texttt{octant\_hull\_geo} 30.0\%
vs.~\texttt{octant\_cbg} 25.2\%). LTD and MTL are negligible by comparison.
Figure: per-phase latency boxplot; accuracy vs. throughput scatter (the two Pareto points are
Monte Carlo and geometric centroid).
VERIFIED from \texttt{rq3\_runtime\_global\_as16509\_final.csv}.]}
